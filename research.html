Research

I am broadly interested in the climate of the polar and high alpine regions, with a focus on snow and ice. In my research, I am using stable water isotopes to better understand snow processes and its interactions with the atmosphere. I have mostly worked with observational data from in-situ measurements or laboratory work. Progressively my work is also including numerical models of increasing complexity.
